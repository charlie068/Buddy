Working after hours in LSB


Everyone who works out-of-hours MUST use the BUDDY signing in system. Failure to do so may result in the withdrawal of access privileges.


The online BUDDY signing-in system is at http://bristolbuddy.x10host.com/

 

Normal working hours are from 0700 to 1900, Monday to Friday. During these hours there is a porter on duty at reception and there are first aiders on the premises. This enables a rapid response in the event that something goes wrong.

  • All those working out of hours are expected to have a good working knowledge of the emergency procedures, since they may be the first to discover something that requires action.
  • A person may work alone in an office, on paperwork, without special permission however a remote buddy will be required.
  • The risk assessment for any work other than office work must have taken account of the increased risk of working out of hours. ONLY those with the requisite skills may work out-of-hours. ONLY those who have secured the agreement of the school safety advisor and PI and completed the requisite OOH form may work out of hours.
  • No-one may work alone in a laboratory area - there must always be someone within calling distance (a buddy) who would know what to do in the event of an emergency.
  • Work on high risk experiments is FORBIDDEN
